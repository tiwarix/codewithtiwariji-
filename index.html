Welcome To R.R. Group Of Institutions

R.R. Group Of Institutions is a private engineering institute that was established in 2008. The institution opens a path for all learners to own their future by offering various undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs. With high learning streams such as management and engineering, it is recognized as one of the Best B.Tech, MBA, and Polytechnic Colleges in Lucknow and the Top Engineering college in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The institute is well-affirmed by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and affiliated with eminent national bodies such as AKTU (Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University) and the Board of Technical Education.

R.R. group of institutions is allocated as ASSOCHAM (Associated Chambers of Commerce and Industry of India) for the best College award and commended with the ‘Prakriti Ratna Award’ and ‘Shiksha Gaurav Samman’ for its quality education in the field of management and technical education, which counts RRGI among the best engineering colleges in Lucknow. The admission criteria of the institute are concluded under the passing marks in the entrance exams for the respective courses of your choice, like JEECUP (Joint Entrance Examination Council, Uttar Pradesh) and NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture).

OUR COURSES

The engineering course is all about analytical work, new inventions, and research. Employers these days look for candidates who are techno-savvy, and an engineering degree equips you with the necessary skills to meet these demands.

Master the art of business with experienced mentors from the industry and be an asset to the industry get placed in top core companies, R.R. Group of Institutions have more than 500 partner companies which offer you a promising future.

A polytechnic education gives you an experience within a dynamic and progressive learning environment. Polytechnic institutes are dedicated to offering a number of professional courses under one roof. Polytechnic courses are ideal for those who wish to build a career in technical and professional fields.

Academic programs

R.R. Group of Institutions has been granting various undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs in the fields of engineering, technology, and management. RRGI is rapidly progressing in its mission of accommodating ‘Value-Based Technical experts’ and cementing itself among the top colleges in Lucknow. 

  • B.Tech Engineering  

RRGI provides engineering programs that train students in the practice of using mathematics, engineering design processes, and natural science to increase their efficiency and productivity. RRGI encompasses students with a broad range of specialized steams in the field of engineering, such as Computer Science and Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering (AI&ML), Computer Science and Design, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Information Technology (IT), Biotechnology, Electrical Engineering, etc., characterizing it as the top BTech colleges in Lucknow.  

  • Management 

RRGI provides well-assured courses that ground the capacity of students to develop management practices and financial strategies to support their plans. The institute provides management courses such as MBA (Master of Business Administration) and streams such as finance and insurance and risk management, which focuses on mitigating financial risks through products like life insurance, health insurance, property insurance, liability insurance, and business interruption insurance, human resource management, international business, marketing, and information technology, making it one of the best MBA colleges in Lucknow.

  • Polytechnic

With its short course duration, skillful learning experience, and better job opportunities, R.R. Group of Institutions is ideal among the Top polytechnic colleges in Lucknow. The institute furnishes Polytechnic or vocational courses for students such as a Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering, a Diploma in Electrical Engineering, a Diploma in Civil Engineering, a Diploma in Production Engineering, a Diploma in Electronics Engineering, etc. These diploma programs primarily concentrate on delivering technical education to students.
